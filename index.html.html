AI Ranking

KOL Ranking, Simplified

TikaDiscover's AI engine classifies every HCP into tiers based on their publication record, clinical trial involvement, conference activity, industry relationships, and treatment volume — customizable to your therapeutic priorities.

Tier 1
Global KOL Published in top journals. Leads significant clinical trials. Speaks at conferences. In organizational leadership positions.
Tier 2
National KOL Strong research portfolio. Working on cutting-edge treatments. Likely involved in clinical trials. Regional and national leaders.
Tier 3
Diagnostic / Treater Excellent diagnostician with extensive field experience. High patient volume. May not publish or participate in trials.
Tier 4
Community HCP Primarily focused on treating patients. Specialists or primary care. Great diagnostic and treatment experience. Majority of doctors.
AI-Powered, Not Static: Rankings are continuously updated as new publications, clinical trials, and payment data become available. DocNexus data flows into TikaDiscover automatically, ensuring your KOL intelligence is always current — not a stale annual snapshot.
